PClub UIET

Software Freedom Day is organized by Programming Club UIET or PCIub for short - is a student-driven society that aims at educating students and to spread selfless knowledge of various domains of Computer Science, organizes hackathons, and celebrate Open-Source. It's more of a community. We teach, we learn and we grow, together.
